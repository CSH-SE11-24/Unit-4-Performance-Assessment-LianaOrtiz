About OVO

OVO, short for October's Very Own, is a renowned Canadian lifestyle brand and record label founded by hip-hop artist Drake, along with Oliver El-Khatib and Noah "40" Shebib, in 2008. Initially starting as a blog to promote Drake's music and projects, OVO quickly evolved into a multifaceted brand that encompasses fashion, music, and lifestyle products. OVO's iconic owl logo has become synonymous with quality and style, adorning a wide range of merchandise from clothing and accessories to collaborative sneaker releases with major footwear brands. Beyond fashion, OVO has also made significant contributions to the music industry, signing and promoting talented artists while maintaining a distinct sound that resonates with fans worldwide. OVO continues to expand its global presence while staying true to its roots of creativity, authenticity, and innovation.

Common Questions abt OVO

The OVO owl logo holds significant symbolism within the brand's identity. The owl is often associated with wisdom, intelligence, and mystery, which aligns with the ethos of OVO. Additionally, the owl is a nocturnal creature, symbolizing the idea of working tirelessly through the night to achieve success, a sentiment that resonates with OVO's founder, Drake, and the brand's dedication to hard work and perseverance. The logo's sleek and minimalist design has also contributed to its widespread recognition and popularity, making it an iconic symbol within streetwear and hip-hop culture.

The history behind the creation of OVO dates back to 2008 when it was founded by hip-hop artist Drake, along with Oliver El-Khatib and Noah "40" Shebib. Initially conceived as a blog to promote Drake's music and projects, OVO quickly evolved into a full-fledged lifestyle brand and record label. The name "October's Very Own" is a reference to Drake's birth month, October, and reflects the brand's personal connection to its founder. As Drake's career skyrocketed, OVO expanded its offerings beyond music to include fashion, merchandise, and collaborations with other artists and brands. The brand's ethos of authenticity, creativity, and innovation has been central to its growth and success, solidifying OVO's position as a cultural powerhouse within the worlds of music, fashion, and beyond.

OVO has made a significant impact on the fashion industry by seamlessly blending streetwear aesthetics with high-end fashion sensibilities. The brand's clothing and merchandise have become highly sought after by fans and fashion enthusiasts alike, with its iconic owl logo and clean designs adorning everything from t-shirts and hoodies to accessories and collaborative sneaker releases. OVO's influence extends beyond its product offerings, as it has successfully created a lifestyle brand that resonates with a diverse audience worldwide. Moreover, OVO's collaborations with other brands and artists have further elevated its status in the fashion world, cementing its position as a trendsetter and innovator in contemporary streetwear culture.
